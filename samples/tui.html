TUI Elements

Press Tab to navigate, Enter to activate

Buttons (no border)

JS:

TUI.addButton({ row, col, label, onActivate })

|

Buttons (with border)

JS:

TUI.addButton({ row, col, label, border: true, onActivate })

|

Prompt

JS:

TUI.addPrompt({ row, col, width, title, onActivate })

|
Tab through elements and press Enter to activate
